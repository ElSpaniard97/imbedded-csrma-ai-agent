Workflow

Designed to align with enterprise change-control practices.

1
Diagnostics (default)
Clarify scope/impact, analyze evidence, provide read-only verification commands and interpretations.
2
Intent gate
Waits for explicit language like "apply", "proceed", "make the change", "run the fix".
3
Approval checklist
Confirms production impact, maintenance window, backups/restore points, and rollback plan.
4
Remediation (after approval)
Provides step-by-step change instructions, plus validation and rollback/backout steps.
Best practice
Paste Diagnostics into the incident ticket first. Add Remediation only if changes are approved.
