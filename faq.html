FAQ

Operational notes and expectations.

Can the GPT run commands on my systems?
No. It provides guidance and interpretation. You remain in control of execution.
Why is remediation approval-gated?
To align with enterprise change-control. Diagnostics is read-only; remediation requires explicit approval.
Can I paste screenshots?
Yes. Paste screenshots, logs, errors, or full scripts. The GPT will extract the key details.
What should I save in tickets?
Save Diagnostics (symptoms, scope, tests, interpretation). Add Remediation (steps, rollback, validation) only if changes are made.
